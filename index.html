OUR SOLUTIONS

1. Revenue Cycle Management

DijiSync RCM Solutions deliver on its promise by combining our deep expertise in process management, highly trained account managers that process claims quickly and accurately, and state of the art digital solutions that transform the entire RCM cycle.With Digitally powered Account Management and highly trained medical billers we get to learn your practice intimately and therefore perform the best service.


2. Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

Our Revenue Intelligence Dashboard helps you uncover the revenue opportunities that will provide the greatest contribution to your bottom line. Instead of providing you with revenue intelligence once the operations have been conducted, we focus on putting live data-feed in your hand enabling you to have a birds' eye view of your entire operations. Revenue Intelligence Dashboard offers practices an entry point to take back control of the reporting and analytics that impact your organization’s revenue cycle. It is designed to provide healthcare provider with increased visibility and insight into key areas of your revenue cycle.


3. Credentialing Services

DijiSync Credentialing team provides a complete credentialing assistance service to you in becoming a network provider with the carriers. We will guide you by performing a deep analysis for your specialty and service. We will provide you with recommendations by performing an analysis for your specialty and service area where guidance is needed. We constantly follow-up with and monitor the payers to ensure applications are processed on time. We work proactively to identify and resolve potential admin issues before they impact your provider reimbursement

OUR SPECIALITIES

1. Surgery

● RCM Challenge

Your billing staff needs to be well-versed in usage of the global surgical packages, modifiers for surgeon roles and same-day procedures, and documentation for unlisted procedures.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync offers expert staff with a collective Surgery related medical billing experience of more than 50 years.

● RCM Challenge

Missing coordination between the benefits and scheuling teams.

● Our Unique Solution

Our Front Desk and Back Office Teams work in water tight coordination, this ensures that the front office staff are able to coodinate in real-time bases with expert billing staff resulting in timely benefits verification.

2. Internal Medicine

● RCM Challenge

Inaccuracy of patient informaion collected.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync can take over the administrative functions of the front office leaving the clinical staff to manage the most important part: Delivering great clinical services to the patients.

● RCM Challenge

The physician may not be aware of the codes he/she can use in addition to the office level codes to achieve higher reimbursements.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync offers a Code Review service by certified coders (frequency could be bi-annual).

● RCM Challenge

Failure to comply with MACRA and MIPS guidelines which decreases the already low reinbursements for Internal Medicine practice.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync EMR is able to pull MACRA and MIPs reports resulting in higher collections.

3. Orthopedic

● RCM Challenge

Ortho practices usually have rehab centers as a part of the practice. The practice would usually bill the office visits along with the rehab codes without correct modifiers which causes a high denial rate.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync EMR highlights the old codes with a different color (Red), which indicates the provider to use a different code in order to be reimbursed.

● RCM Challenge

When an orthopedics and spine practice adds an ancillary service like physical therapy or in-house MRI scanning, it's helpful to know the administrative rules that each plan has for these services, such as a pre-authorization requirement. Otherwise, the practice will only learn this when it starts receiving denials.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync Front Office Staff combs through the CPT Codes to ensure if a prior authorization is required for each service rendered by the physician.

● RCM Challenge

Most patients seen at the your practice may either be recovering from a surgery or enrolled in hospice. Not knowing about similar circumstances may result in total denials of claims after the patient has been seen.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync offers structured EMR training resulting in fewer mistakes hence lower rejection rates.

4. Cardiology

● RCM Challenge

Cardiologists often like other speacialists struggle with collecting the full allowed amount from the insurance. This also includes the patient payments.

● Our Unique Solution

When the practice subscribes to our front office services along with Billing we give reminder calls to the patients before their visits to make sure to bring in the outstanding balance at the time of their visit.

● RCM Challenge

Billing staff must have a thorough understanding of the appropriate codes and modifiers required for cardiology procedures like pacemakers, different types of percutaneous coronary interventions, and peripheral vascular procedures.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync offers expert staff with a collective Cardiology related medical billing experience of more than 50 years.

● RCM Challenge

Most busy specialties such as cardiologist do not keep a tab on their licensure or paticipation expirations. This is a credentialing function where a doctor knows he/she is credentialed but doesnt realise that they have recertify their information with the paypr atleast once every year or every three years.

● Our Unique Solution

While we work on denials we ensure that any denials we get for the provider being non-par with the insurance company are dealt with by sending the request to our credentialing department to fulfil the credentialing requirement.

5. Gastroenterology

● RCM Challenge

As a gastroenterologist, the provider works with patients who have a wide range of healthcare needs, from consultative visits to procedures requiring anesthesia like colonoscopies and surgery. As a result, billing can become complex, particularly when dealing with issues like underpayments or claim denials.

● Our Unique Solution

DijiSync deploys medical billers and coders that have the most indepth experience in working with Gastroentrologists ensuring that all the correct procedures are followed in order to maintain and increase the reimbursements.

● RCM Challenge

Gastroenterology is one of the fastest evolving branch in healthcare, the codes and testing evolve just as quickly. Physicians need to be constantly updated on new testing, equipment and their codes.

● Our Unique Solution

Our digital solutions will keep you abreast of all the news and evolution surrounding your specialty. Our team of experts will be committed to sharing the latest in healthcare innovations along with the application to your practice.
