OUR SOLUTIONS

1. Revenue Cycle Management

DijiSync RCM Solutions deliver on its promise by combining our deep expertise in process management, highly trained account managers that process claims quickly and accurately, and state of the art digital solutions that transform the entire RCM cycle.With Digitally powered Account Management and highly trained medical billers we get to learn your practice intimately and therefore perform the best service.


2. Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

Our Revenue Intelligence Dashboard helps you uncover the revenue opportunities that will provide the greatest contribution to your bottom line. Instead of providing you with revenue intelligence once the operations have been conducted, we focus on putting live data-feed in your hand enabling you to have a birds' eye view of your entire operations. Revenue Intelligence Dashboard offers practices an entry point to take back control of the reporting and analytics that impact your organization’s revenue cycle. It is designed to provide healthcare provider with increased visibility and insight into key areas of your revenue cycle.


3. Credentialing Services

DijiSync Credentialing team provides a complete credentialing assistance service to you in becoming a network provider with the carriers. We will guide you by performing a deep analysis for your specialty and service. We will provide you with recommendations by performing an analysis for your specialty and service area where guidance is needed. We constantly follow-up with and monitor the payers to ensure applications are processed on time. We work proactively to identify and resolve potential admin issues before they impact your provider reimbursement
